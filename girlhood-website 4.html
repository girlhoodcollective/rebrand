Our Story

Built for the girls who deserve more.

"Girlhood deserves to be taken seriously — and so does the community that forms around it."

Girlhood Collective was built because girlhood isn't something to rush through. It's a foundation. The things a girl learns about herself at nine, twelve, sixteen — the way she learns to trust her instincts, take creative risks, show up for the people next to her — those things don't stay in childhood. They become who she is for the rest of her life.

We are a studio, a community, and a gathering place for girls and women at every stage. Not just kids. Not just adults. All of us, together. Because the most powerful thing about girlhood is that it connects generations — and we think that's worth building a space around.
